About Bank of Georgia

Bank of Georgia is a leading company in terms of loan portfolios, deposits, assets, and stock shares.
It has a wide network of service centers and ATMs throughout the country and also
has representative offices in London, Budapest, and Tel-Aviv. It is one of the largest employers in Georgia.
Since its foundation, Bank of Georgia continues to emphasize several important directions: Education, the environment, and other important social issues.



The world is changing fast and it is crucial to be an innovator in order to keep pace with world dynamics.
For several decades, Bank of Georgia has been continuously trying to introduce and implement products
and services that are essential for the customers, innovative in the field of banking, and important for the country.
Bank of Georgias mission is to support the customers, investors, employees, and public by professionalism,
commitment, and constant innovation to create a successful future together.



Bank of Georgia boasts more than 2 000 0000 customers in Georgia, which proves that the company has been developing in the right direction.
2 000 000 people means the same number of different values, expectations, and wishes and this is a great challenge and responsibility on its own.
From day one, Bank of Georgia has tried to make bank services accessible to everyone.
Customers with their individual wishes and goals are the ones who used to be and continue to be at the center of our service.
The bank continues trying to meet all of our clients needs at any particular moment.
